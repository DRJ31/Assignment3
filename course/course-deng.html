Whole Person Education

Author: DengRenjie(Jack)

Whole Person Education(acronym:WPE),focus on every student's intelligence, moral, physical fitness, social, emotion and aesthetic. Our project is set up to help students form their character, learn skills which can be used in their future life, gain more knowledge about social skills and can think by themselves and solve their personal problems.Last but not least, they can own lofty moral awareness and social awareness.

Whole Person Education Office(acronym:WPEO)was set up in July, 2006, and develop Whole Person Education Courses by setting up a series of experience learing module. WPE learning module is carriy out through classses, and instructed by all the centers in WPEO. WPEO also provides various kinds of programs which can enhance the experience of students.

Now WPEO has 6 centers, including:AEC, EIDC, ACDC, VSDC, EDC and PEC.

      Features

· Experience More

WPE provides various kinds of activities for us to experience, which can make us learn more and gain more experience.

· Build Characters

In WPE courses, students can not only experience various kinds of activities, but also form their character. By participating with team members and take part in some activities, students can build a good character which will benefit them in their future life.

· Learn Abilities

In WPE classes, students are required to finish some targets. By finishing these targets, students can gain their ability of communicating with people, think independently and other abilities.

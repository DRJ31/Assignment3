IT for Success of Everyday Life and Work

Author: WuZhenghao(Jack)

With the rapid development of Computer Science and Information Technology, Computer based education has become more and more important for college student. Akk student need to have the necessary infomation literacy and IT skills, as well as numerical and problem-solving skills, to function effectively in work and everyday life.

To meet this requirement, many schools have revised their syllabi to keep the content up-to-date. As a Liberal Arts College, UIC intends to cultivate outstanding student who can use computeras a tool to work productively and bring convenience to everyday tasks.
